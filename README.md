# 🚀 AI Supply Chain Intelligence Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

The AI Supply Chain Intelligence Platform is an end-to-end intelligent logistics and supply chain analytics solution built using Machine Learning, Generative AI, PostgreSQL, Power BI, and Streamlit.

The platform helps businesses predict shipment delays, forecast future demand, analyze operational performance, assess weather-related shipment risks, and interact with an AI-powered supply chain assistant.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 📦 Shipment Delay Prediction

* Predicts whether a shipment will be delayed or delivered on time.
* Machine Learning model trained on supply chain data.
* Displays prediction confidence scores.
* Stores prediction results in PostgreSQL.

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard

* Interactive shipment analytics.
* Total shipments tracking.
* Delay percentage monitoring.
* Warehouse-wise shipment distribution.
* Shipment status visualization.

### 📈 Demand Forecasting

* Forecasts future product demand.
* Helps optimize inventory planning.
* Visual demand trend analysis.

### 🌦 Weather Delay Risk Analysis

* Real-time weather data integration.
* Evaluates shipment delay risks based on weather conditions.
* Risk categorization:

  * Low Risk
  * Medium Risk
  * High Risk
* Stores weather records in PostgreSQL.

### 🧠 AI Supply Chain Assistant (RAG + Gemini)

* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture.
* Uses custom knowledge base.
* Powered by Google Gemini.
* Answers supply chain-related questions intelligently.
